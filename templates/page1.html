Pranamasana

Pranamasana is a centering asana often used as a routine part of Surya Namaskar or as a transitional pose. While standing, sitting or squatting, the palms press together in front of the heartâ€™s center while allowing the shoulders to roll down the back and the elbows to rest at the sides of the rib cage. Pranamasana is also known as prayer pose in English.

Tutorial

This is a basic prayer stance. Stand at the edge of the mat keeping your feet together and balance your weight evenly on both feet. As you breathe in, lift both arms from the sides, and as you exhale bring your palms together in front of the chest in a prayer position.
